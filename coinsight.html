CoinSight is a cryptocurrency portfolio manager that provides key market insights as well as up to date price tracking. CoinSight helps users learn more about the cryptocurrency market and make informed decisions by uniquely aggregating sentiment data across popular platforms that host cryptocurrency discussions such as Twitter, Reddit and various news sources.
